Our Collections

What we make

Every creation is made entirely by hand, to order. No batch production — just dedicated, personal craftsmanship.

🎁

Gift Boxes

Luxuriously presented boxes of hand-crafted chocolates. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and special moments. Choose your flavours, quantities, and personalise your message card.

💍

Wedding Favours

Elegant, personalised chocolate favours that double as keepsakes. Custom branding, names, or dates on each piece — a beautiful memory your guests will take home.

🏢

Corporate Hampers

Impress clients and reward your team with bespoke branded hampers. Logo packaging, tailored flavour profiles, and bulk order pricing available on request.

🌹

Truffles

Silky ganache truffles in rotating seasonal flavours. Think champagne & raspberry, sea salt caramel, spiced chai, or pistachio rose. Sold by the box or individually.

🎂

Celebration Bars

Large statement chocolate bars dressed with edible flowers, gold leaf, and hand-piped personalised messages. A showstopping centrepiece gift for any occasion.

✨

Fully Bespoke

Have a completely unique vision? We welcome it. Get in touch with your idea and we'll collaborate with you to bring your dream chocolate creation to life from scratch.
