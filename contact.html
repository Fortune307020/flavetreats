FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend ordering at least 5–7 days in advance for standard boxes, and 2–4 weeks for wedding or large corporate orders. Rush orders may be possible — just ask!

Can I choose my own flavours?

Absolutely. All orders are made to your preferences. You can mix and match from our current flavour range, and we'll let you know if any seasonal specials are available.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer free local delivery within Corby and surrounding areas. Nationwide shipping is available at a small additional cost. Please mention your delivery postcode when ordering.

Do you cater for dietary requirements?

We can accommodate vegan, dairy-free, and gluten-free requests for most collections. Please always mention any allergies or dietary needs in your order and we'll do our best to accommodate.

How do I pay?

Once we've confirmed your order via email, we'll send payment details. We accept bank transfer and most major payment methods. A deposit may be required for large orders.
